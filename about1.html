ABOUT THE COMPANY

PTEC is an international standard hygienic process equipment manufacturing ,Trading and contracting company based in India .we are a professionally managed company with hands on strong professional and technical team with all aspects of engineering needs such as trading ,Design & Engineering , Execution , Manufacturing ,Project Management & Commissioning with Electrical & Automation Supports for Industrial Breweries, Industrial craft Breweries ,Micro Breweries ,Distillery, Edible Oil Refineries, WTP & Cross Country Piping for water Projects Etc .

Implementing those values has earned us the trust of our many valued clients.
